Web development involves the creation, building, and maintenance of websites and web applications that run on the internet. It encompasses a wide range of tasks, including coding, technical design, and ensuring the performance of a website or application.

Web development refers to the process of designing, building, and maintaining websites or web applications. A web developer is responsible for ensuring that a site is visually appealing, functional, and user-friendly. This work can be split into three main areas: front-end development (user interface and design), back-end development (server-side logic and databases), and full-stack development (both front-end and back-end) .
